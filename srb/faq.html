Q. How good are your bottles?

A. We use World-Class Used Bottlery in all of our Unique and Varied Products.

Q. Can they be used to rebottle alcohol?

A. Due to Legal Issues, we have not been able to Rebottle your Various Alcohols. If you would like this to change, please send Ten (10) to One-Hundred (100) Snail Mail Messages to your Local Senator on why Alcohol Bottling should not need a Pre-Ordained License.

Q. Are you liable for damages if your products break?

A. We are not Liable for any Damages you Incur while using this Product. All liability is Limited to Compensation via a Single-Use Redemption Daytime Fun Time Ticket which can be redeemed at any Six Flags Carnival. This Offer is Limited to One (1) per Person.

Q. Will you bottle any meat I have?

A. Meat Bottling is a Liability due to the Risk of the creation of High Meat, which is a Dangerous Meat Product that Causes Cancer for those who Eat it in California. Therefore, you must sign a Waiver that Revokes your Privilege to obtain a Single-Use Redemption Daytime Fun Time Ticket for any Violation of the Law you may encounter upon Usage of our Services.

Q. What about fruits or crackers?

Fruits and Crackers are fine to Rebottle as long as they are Healthy and Ripe. Make sure to Store them in the Refrigerator for Safe Consumption.
